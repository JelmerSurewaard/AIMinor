What's your mood?

Check my mood

Your mood is important!

Your mood, among other things, determines how you are perceived by others. 
you can use this test to determine which mood your face radiates. Do you have something big coming up? Like an interview for a new job, or are you getting ready for a date? Check your mood now to see what mood you have!

How is your mood calculated?

The picture you upload will be scanned and served to our AI. This AI is trained with 57000+ images of people's faces. It then classifies the image depending on what mood it finds on the face. This is done by so called "feature extraction" to determine what kind of mood is depicted on the face. Currently, our model can 92% of the time accuratly determine the mood of the given pictures. The better the face is shown in the picture, the more accurate the AI will be! 

What will happen with my picture?


Absolutely nothing! The picture you upload will only be used by the AI. It is automaticly deleted form our servers. This way your picture will not be found anywhere else on the internet!
